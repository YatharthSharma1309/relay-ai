# Relay AI Demo FAQ

## Refund Policy
Customers can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase. After 14 days, refunds are reviewed case by case.

## Billing
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal. Invoices are sent on the 1st of each month.

## Password Reset
Go to Settings > Security > Reset Password. A reset link is emailed within 5 minutes.

## API Rate Limits
Free plan: 100 requests/day. Pro plan: 10,000 requests/day. Enterprise: custom limits.

## Support Hours
Live support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. AI chat is available 24/7.

## Escalation
If the AI cannot resolve your issue, click "Escalate to ticket" and an agent will respond within 24 hours.
